Momos

Description:

Momos are a type of dumpling native to Nepal, Tibet, and parts of India. They are typically filled with spiced meat or vegetables and served with a dipping sauce.

Ingredients:

For the filling:

  • 250g ground chicken or vegetables (cabbage, carrot, and green peas)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, minced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the wrapping:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • Water, as needed

For the dipping sauce:

  • 4 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon chili sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil

Steps:

  1. Prepare the filling by saut√©ing onion, garlic, and ginger in oil until fragrant. Add ground chicken or vegetables, soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Cook until the filling is cooked through. Allow it to cool.
  2. Make the dough for wrappers by mixing flour with water until it forms a smooth, elastic dough. Let it rest for 30 minutes.
  3. Roll out the dough into thin circles and place a spoonful of filling in the center of each circle. Fold the edges to form dumplings.
  4. Steam the momos for about 10-15 minutes until cooked through. Mix all the ingredients for the dipping sauce and serve with the cooked momos.
