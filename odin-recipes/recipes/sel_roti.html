Sel Roti

Description:

Sel Roti is a traditional Nepali sweet bread ring-shaped, made from rice flour and deep-fried. It's often prepared during festivals and special occasions.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups rice flour
  • 1 cup ripe mashed bananas
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • Oil for frying

Steps:

  1. In a bowl, mix rice flour, mashed bananas, sugar, and cardamom powder to form a thick batter.
  2. Heat oil in a deep frying pan.
  3. Pour a ladleful of batter into the hot oil in a circular motion to form a ring shape.
  4. Fry until golden brown on both sides.
  5. Remove from oil and drain excess oil on paper towels.
  6. Serve hot or at room temperature.
